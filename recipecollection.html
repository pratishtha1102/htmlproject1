Chocolate Cake

Today I am going to share chocolate cake recepie, which is my favourite, for chocolate cake you need to following ingredients

Ingredients
Item Quantity
All-purpose flour 250 gms
Cocoa powder 50 g
Fine sugar 150 g
Teaspoons baking powder 5g
Salt 1 teaspoon
Eggs 2 large
Whole milk 1 cup
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup
Pure vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
Water 1 cup

Process

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine:
    • 250g all-purpose flour
    • 50g cocoa powder
    • 150g fine sugar
    • 5g baking powder
    • 5g baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon salt

  3. In a separate bowl, beat 2 large eggs, and then add:
    • 240ml (1 cup) whole milk
    • 120ml (1/2 cup) vegetable oil
    • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
    • Mix the wet ingredients well.

  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be cautious not to overmix.

  5. Gradually add 240ml (1 cup) of water to the batter, stirring until smooth. The batter may appear thin, but that's normal.

  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans and bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick
    inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Once the cakes are cooled, you can frost and decorate them according to your preference. Whether it's a rich chocolate ganache,
    creamy buttercream, or any other favorite frosting, this chocolate cake is ready to be enjoyed!
